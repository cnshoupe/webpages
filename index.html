Hi! My name is Caleb Shoupe and I am junior majoring in Civil Engineering. I chose this major for several reasons: the high demand for engineers and large workload in this field, the fact that I have always enjoyed observing this type of work being done, and the fact that I can make a good financial living in this field. After believing this was a field that I would enjoy working while while making a good living I interned for several summers with NCDOT and decided that was indeed the path for me to follow.


I am not currently working a job but I have interned the past two summers with the NCDOT in Asheville NC. I really enjoyed these summers as I was able to gain lots of valuable experience while making money school and multiple professional contacts and references. I will probably seek out an internship with another company this summer so that I can get experience in other areas of Civil Engineering seeing as it is a very broad field with many possibilities.
